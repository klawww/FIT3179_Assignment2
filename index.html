Fast Food Restaurants Distribution Across America

Visualisations among this page show the distribution and ranking of fast food brands and different states.

by Guangyu Tian

5 Most Fast Food Restaurants

This dot map shows the distribution of 5 fast food brands that have the most restaurants across America.
The category on the right side can be selected by clicking/shift clicking the restaurant name(s).

Fast Fodd Restaurants per capital

The map shows the distribution of fast food restaurants across America per 100,000 population.

Filter can be applied by dragging the slider to filter the minimum population for the states.

Restaurants By Brands/States

The left chart shows 10 states that contain the most Subway. A filter is also applied to the selection for each state.

The right chart shows 10 brands that contain the most restaurants. A filter is also applied by the minimum number of restaurants.

Restaurants By States For Brands

A combination of two bar charts above, we can compare the total number of restaurants of the four brands in the states.

State selection as the filter can also be applied to this chart.

Subway & McDonals's In Each State

This plot shows the number of Subway and McDonald's in each state which is categorised by regions.

Filter can be applied by dragging the slider to filter the minimum population for the states, selection on the legend or the box can also apply filter on the regions.

We can see that California and Ohio have the Subway and McDonald's respectively, New York and Pennsylvania are the only states in Northeast that have Subway and MocDonald's. Besides, Alaska and Wiscosin contain basically the same number of Subway but quite different in population.

This visualisation is created by Guangyu Tian. The datasource is some data repository online.
