Fast Food Restaurants Distribution Across America

Visualisations among this page show the distribution and ranking of fast food brands and different states.

by Guangyu Tian

5 Most Fast Food Restaurants

This dot map shows the distribution of 5 fast food brands which are Subway, McDonald's, Taco Bell, Pizza Hut, Bueger Kind that have the most restaurants across America.

All the restaurants mainly located in middle and the south of America. As for the west, restaurants are mainly located in California

Among the 5 brands, Subway has the most restaurants and Bueger Kind has the least restaurants

The legend on the right side can be selected by clicking/shift clicking the restaurant name(s).

Fast Food Restaurants per capital

The map shows the distribution of fast food restaurants across America per 500,000 population.

Alabama contains the most fast food restaurants per capital which is 64.8. Besides, West Virginia and Alaska also have more than 40 restaurants per capital.
However, ther are three states do not have any fast food restaurant, which are Vermont, New Hampshire and Maine

Filter can be applied by dragging the slider to filter the minimum population for the states.

Subway & McDonald's In Each State

This plot shows the number of Subway and McDonald's in each state which is categorised by regions.

We can see that California and Ohio have the most Subway and McDonald's respectively, New York and Pennsylvania are the only states in Northeast that have Subway and McDonald's.
Besides, Alaska and Wiscosin contain basically the same number of Subway but quite different in population.

Filter can be applied by dragging the slider to filter the minimum population for the states, selection on the legend or the box can also apply filter on the regions.

Restaurants By Brands/States

The left chart shows 10 brands that contain the most restaurants. The brands that contain most restaurants in America are Subway, McDonald's, Taco Bell, Pizza Hut, Bueger Kind.

The right chart shows 10 states that contain the most Subway. There are four states in Midwest and the states contain the most and second most Subway are Ohio and Indiana.
There is no state from Northeast included in this chart.

Restaurants By States Of Brands

A combination of two bar charts above, we can compare the total number of restaurants of the four brands in the states.

California and Florida have the most Bueger Kind,
California also contains the most McDonald's and Taco Bell and Ohio has the most Pizza Hut and Subway.

State selection as the filter can also be applied to this chart.
